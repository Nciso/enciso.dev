## Who I am

I'm Enrique Enciso — a software engineer and startup founder. I'm the CTO and
co-founder of **[Jaro](/projects/jaro)**, the company building
**[Reley](/projects/reley)**, a platform that collects and verifies the documents
businesses need from each other, built for AI agents. I care about systems that
are private by default, cheap to run, and honest about what they know.

## Why I'm the right person to build Reley

Reley is a verification company, and trust and verification between businesses is
what I've worked on for a decade — before it had that name. At AutoCloud I built
the SCIM and SSO layer; at Flourish I designed bank-grade multitenancy so
different banks could safely share a platform; I advised Mexican fintechs on
SOC 2, HIPAA, and PCI compliance. Then at Jaro the same problem showed up in the
physical economy: no freight or factoring deal closed until we could collect and
verify the counterparty's documents. Reley is where that whole thread converges —
at the moment AI agents start running these workflows and need verifiable inputs
and a record of every decision. See [Jaro](/projects/jaro) for how we got here.

## What I'm building

I'm the technical half of a two-person founding team: I own product and
engineering, and my co-founder **Jafeth Vázquez Navarro (CEO)** owns go-to-market
and commercial — the split that's put Reley in front of real logistics and finance
customers. I'm interested in the shift from software people operate by hand to
workflows AI agents can run safely — this very site is a small working example of
grounded, verifiable answers over guesswork.

## How I think about engineering

I optimize for zero recurring cost and zero surprise. I like static hosting,
build-time work over runtime work, and pushing computation to the edge or the
client whenever it keeps user data private. If a feature can run in the
visitor's browser instead of a server I own, I'll usually choose the browser.

## What I value

- **Grounded answers over confident guesses.** Every claim should trace to a
  source.
- **Private by default.** Data shouldn't leave a device unless the user asks.
- **Boring infrastructure.** The best backend is often no backend.
